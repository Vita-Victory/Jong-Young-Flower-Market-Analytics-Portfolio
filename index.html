Case Study Methodology

Data Collection & Mining

Automated retrieval of verified customer reviews via custom Python scrapers. Extraction of raw rating curves and chronological metrics.

Sentiment NLP Parsing

Classification of textual nuances into Positive, Neutral, and Negative segments. Theme association to trace service bottlenecks and product value drivers.

Financial Modeling

Formulation of core unit economics, seasonality curves, and marketing acquisition costs. Translating operational gaps into quantifiable pricing variables.

Business Strategy & Dashboarding

Synthesizing visual dashboards and flexible simulators. Building executive recommendations with projections, budgeting bounds, and ROI paths.
